Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2, 2026

LearnTech Games ("we", "us", or "our") built Chess Master Quest — Idle (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how we use it, and what choices you have. We've written this in plain English so it's easy to understand.

1. Information We Collect

We designed the App to collect as little data as possible. Here is exactly what is collected and why:

Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how players use the App — for example, which features are popular and where players get stuck. Firebase Analytics collects:

  • Anonymous app usage events (app opens, screen views, gameplay events)
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Google may collect device advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID / iOS IDFA) and IP address as part of Analytics processing

No user-identifying information (such as name or email) is sent by the App to Analytics. For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Crash Reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

We use Firebase Crashlytics to detect and fix crashes. When the App crashes, Crashlytics collects:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Stack traces and app state at the time of the crash
  • A Crashlytics installation UUID (not tied to your identity)

No personally identifiable information is intentionally included in crash reports.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional one-time purchases (such as a premium unlock and cosmetic bundles). All purchase transactions are processed entirely by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not directly collect or store your payment information. Purchase entitlements may be verified server-side via Firebase Functions.

Local Storage

Game save data, settings, tutorial progress, and audio preferences are stored locally on your device only. No account creation is required and no cloud sync currently exists. This data never leaves your device.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No ads — the App does not use any advertising SDK and does not display ads.
  • No special permissions — the App does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other sensitive device features. Only the INTERNET permission is used (required by Firebase).
  • No user-generated content — players cannot create, share, or upload content.
  • No account system — there is no login, no email collection via the App, no social features, and no user profiles synced to a server.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used solely to:

  • Fix crashes and bugs — Crashlytics data helps us identify and resolve technical issues quickly.
  • Improve the game — Analytics data helps us understand which features players enjoy, where difficulty spikes occur, and how to make the game better.
  • Verify purchases — purchase entitlements are validated to ensure players receive what they paid for.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google / Firebase — Analytics, Crashlytics, and purchase verification
  • Google Play — in-app purchase processing (Android)
  • Apple — in-app purchase processing (iOS)

5. Data Retention

Analytics and Crashlytics data is retained according to Firebase's default retention policies. Analytics data is retained for up to 14 months. Crash data is retained until manually deleted. We may delete data sooner if it is no longer needed.

6. Your Rights

You can request that we delete any data associated with your device by contacting us at learntechgames@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

On Android, you can opt out of personalized analytics by resetting or deleting your Advertising ID in your device settings. On iOS, you can limit ad tracking via Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

7. Children's Privacy

Chess Master Quest — Idle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at learntechgames@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
